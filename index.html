What is KernelCoin?

KernelCoin is a custom digital token created for KernelCon attendees. It represents access, rewards, and participation in the KernelCon ecosystem. Whether you're attending training, participating in CTFs, or engaging with speakers and vendors, KernelCoin is your badge of honour for this year’s event.

Token Utilities

  • Rewards in the CTF and hacker villages.
  • Special discounts or freebies from vendor partners at the show.
  • A commemorative digital badge you can keep as a memory of the conference.
